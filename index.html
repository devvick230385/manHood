A new culture starts with your

The truth is that we did not set out to write a book. This book is a result of our efforts to give what we wished we had. Our goal here is to see a world where men are empowered to take their maleness as a resource in the hands of the prudent steward, creating experiences that make our world a much better place.

This book seeks a world, the men in your life and our communities are empowered to grow their vision, expectations and aspirations of what it means to be a man. It is also a world where the change we seek in our men and the relationship with our men are empowering us to all & collectively celebrate.

This is for the battered, the beaten, the latecomers, the everyday people plagued with the travails of everyday life. Yet, still resilient enough to see a forest in a single grain of thought.

This is to those whose traumatic life experiences have been unable to steal their dreams.

To those who get back up unbroken by their many failings, flattering and falling.

To those who keep growing until they manifest those thoughts and products within, our world is a better place because of you.
